## Overview

A modern marketplace connecting customers with independent artisan bakeries through a seamless online ordering experience. The platform enables bakery discovery, customizable product ordering, inventory-aware purchases, and efficient order management while preserving each bakery's unique identity.

## Challenges

Most local bakeries rely on fragmented ordering methods like WhatsApp, phone calls, or walk-in purchases. Customers have limited visibility into product availability, customization options, and delivery status, while bakery owners struggle with inventory management and digital storefronts.

## Architecture

A React frontend communicates with a Node.js and Express REST API backed by MongoDB. Product catalogs, carts, and inventory validation stay in sync across customer and bakery-facing surfaces.

## Development Process

As Frontend Developer: reusable React components, responsive marketplace UI, product browsing, cart interactions, authentication flows, REST API integration, state management, and performance-minded rendering for product catalogs.

## Results

Delivered a polished marketplace experience enabling customers to discover artisan bakeries, customize products, and complete purchases while giving bakery owners a scalable digital storefront.

## Technologies

React, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB.

## Reflection

Marketplace products succeed when inventory truth and customization feel invisible to the customer. Next steps would deepen bakery-specific branding within a still-cohesive platform shell.
